Coach Form

Add a team and jersey-only roster.

This form stores records in the ERD-style Team, TeamCoach, and Player tables. Player records use jersey number only for roster privacy.

Team information

The league rules come from the seeded League table. The current signed-in coach is linked through TeamCoach.

Roster privacy rule: do not enter player names. Each roster row stores only team_id, jersey_number, default_status, and is_active.

Jersey roster rows

Enter jersey numbers only. Blank roster slots are ignored. Duplicate jersey numbers inside this team are blocked. Another team can still use the same jersey number.
